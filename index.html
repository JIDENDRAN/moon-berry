About Moon Berry

Moon Berry is more than just a brand — it is a little world created with love, creativity, and happiness. Founded by Kanchan Katara and Aashima Mehta, Moon Berry began as a simple conversation between two friends who shared a dream of building something meaningful together. What started over a casual cup of coffee slowly grew into a beautiful journey filled with passion, imagination, and the desire to spread smiles through thoughtful and unique products.

At Moon Berry, we believe that the little things in life can bring the greatest joy. Our inspiration comes from the happiness people feel when they discover something cute, colorful, and special. From trendy stationery, adorable keychains, and stylish lunch boxes to kids’ accessories, travel essentials, and carefully curated gift hampers, every product is selected with care and love. We wanted to create a space where children, students, and anyone who enjoys creative and charming products can find something that truly makes them happy.

We also believe that everyday essentials should reflect personality and style rather than being ordinary or boring. That is why we focus on offering unique, high-quality, and trendy products that add creativity and excitement to daily life. Every item at Moon Berry is designed to make gifting more memorable and everyday moments more special.

As a growing small business, every order and every message means so much to us. The love and support we receive motivate us to continue dreaming bigger and bringing more happiness to our customers. Our journey is not only about selling products but also about creating joyful experiences and meaningful memories.

Thank you for being a part of the Moon Berry family. Together, let’s make every celebration brighter, every gift more special, and every day a little cuter with Moon Berry.

About Our CEO


Kanchan Katara is a mother of two and has been based in Dubai for over 20 years, building a life shaped by resilience, dedication, and quiet ambition. With a long-held vision to create something of her own, she has grown through every challenge with strength and self-belief. Her journey reflects perseverance and purpose, transforming life experiences into motivation to build something meaningful. Moon Berry is the result of that vision — a brand created with heart, elegance, and intention, reflecting her belief in growth, creativity, and the beauty of everyday moments.

Aashima Mehta is a mother of two and has been based in Dubai for the past 10 years, building her journey with passion, creativity, and determination. With a strong entrepreneurial spirit and a love for thoughtful design, she has always believed in creating something meaningful that brings joy to everyday life. Her journey reflects dedication, growth, and the courage to turn ideas into reality. Inspired by creativity and modern lifestyle trends, she co-founded Moon Berry with the vision of offering products that are stylish, fun, and thoughtfully curated. Through Moon Berry, Aashima brings together elegance, imagination, and warmth — creating a brand that celebrates happiness in the little things.
